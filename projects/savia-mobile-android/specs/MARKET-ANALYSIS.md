### Gap Analysis
No existing app combines: AI conversational interface + PM-specific expertise + self-hosted workspace integration + open-source philosophy. Savia Mobile fills this gap.

## 2. Target Market Size

- Global project management software market: $7.8B (2025), growing 10.7% CAGR
- Mobile workforce: 60% of PMs use phones for work daily
- AI adoption in PM: 35% of teams using AI tools (2026)
- Addressable market: ~2.1M potential users (technical PMs seeking AI tools)

## 3. Differentiation

1. **Open-source workspace**: Unlike proprietary tools, users own their data and configuration
2. **Self-hosted option**: SSH tunnel to user's own pm-workspace
3. **AI-native**: Built around AI conversation, not retrofitted
4. **PM personality**: Savia understands Agile, sprints, risk management natively
5. **Bilingual**: Spanish and English from day one
6. **No vendor lock-in**: Works with Claude API, expandable to other LLMs
